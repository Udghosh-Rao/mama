About Jai Guru Dev Spun Pipe

Your Trusted Partner in Quality RCC Products

Leading RCC Pipe Manufacturer in Rewari

Established in 2020, Jai Guru Dev Spun Pipe has rapidly become a trusted name in the RCC pipe manufacturing industry across Haryana, Delhi NCR, and Rajasthan. We specialize in producing high-quality reinforced concrete cement pipes using advanced spinning technology.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Rewari is equipped with modern machinery and adheres to strict quality control measures. We are proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring that every product meets international standards.

We serve diverse sectors including government infrastructure projects, private construction, agriculture, water supply systems, and industrial drainage networks.
