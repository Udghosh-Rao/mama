About Jai Guru Dev Spun Pipe

We are a premier manufacturer of RCC Spun Pipes based in Rewari, Haryana. With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and stringent quality control measures, we deliver superior concrete pipes for various infrastructure projects.

Our pipes are manufactured using advanced spinning technology, ensuring uniform concrete distribution, high strength, and durability. We cater to government projects, private contractors, and industrial establishments.

Committed to excellence, we maintain ISO standards and BIS certifications for all our products. Our experienced team ensures timely delivery and comprehensive technical support.
