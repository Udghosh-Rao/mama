About Our Company

Leading RCC Pipe Manufacturer Since 2020

Delivering superior quality reinforced concrete cement pipes and concrete products with cutting-edge technology and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Your Trusted Partner in Quality Infrastructure

Jai Guru Dev Spun Pipe stands as a premier manufacturer of RCC Spun Pipes, Hume Pipes, and comprehensive concrete products in Rewari, Haryana. Since our establishment in 2020, we have rapidly evolved into a trusted name across Delhi NCR, Haryana, and Rajasthan regions.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced spinning technology that ensures uniform concrete distribution, superior strength, and exceptional durability. We maintain strict adherence to ISO 9001:2015 quality management systems and BIS standards.

We proudly serve diverse sectors including government infrastructure projects, private construction companies, agricultural irrigation systems, municipal water supply networks, and industrial drainage solutions.
